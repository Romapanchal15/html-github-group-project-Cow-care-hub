Introduction

CowCare Hub is a complete informational website created as an HTML student project. It provides detailed knowledge about cow care, indigenous cow breeds, cow diseases, services, and the importance of cows in Indian agriculture and culture.

The main goal of this project is to spread awareness about proper cow management, animal welfare and sustainable farming practices.

About CowCare Hub

CowCare Hub focuses on promoting cow protection and ethical treatment of animals. Cows are an important part of rural economy and dairy industry.

  • Awareness about cow welfare
  • Support to farmers and dairy owners
  • Preservation of indigenous cow breeds

Proper Cow Care

Proper care of cows ensures good health, higher milk production and longer lifespan. CowCare Hub promotes the following care practices:

  • Clean and dry shelter
  • Fresh fodder and clean water
  • Regular medical check-ups
  • Humane and kind treatment

Indian Cow Breeds

India is home to many indigenous cow breeds which are known for A2 milk and disease resistance.

Breed Origin Milk Yield
Gir Gujarat 10–15 Litres
Sahiwal Punjab 8–12 Litres
Tharparkar Rajasthan 8–10 Litres
Red Sindhi Sindh Region 6–10 Litres

Common Cow Diseases

Cows can suffer from various diseases. Early identification and treatment is very important.

  • Foot and Mouth Disease
  • Mastitis
  • Milk Fever
  • Skin Infections
